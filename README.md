# Placement-management-System
core features :

Student :
-View the job according to their CGPA.
-See the available jobs.
-Apply to the job.
-See the Interview Schedule.
-Track the status.

Recruiter :
-posting job.
-Scheduling interview for the students who applied for the job.
-Pass or fail a student in a particular round.
-View Analytics of students.

Admin :
-View total students, companies on dashboard.
-Approve the jobs posted by recruiter.
-Deactivate users if needed (Includes deactivation of students and recruiters).
-View Placement Analytics.
-View Company reports of Recruiters.
-View Branch reports of Students

AI chatbot assistance :
-Helps in assisting the users to navigate through the website at each step
